## Open Data Portals

**Switzerland's central government data repositories and official statistical sources.**

- **[Federal Statistical Office (FSO)](https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/en/home/services/ogd.html)**: National statistics, demographics, economy, society data
- **[opendata.swiss](https://opendata.swiss)**: Central portal for Swiss open government data (140+ organizations, 11,000+ datasets). [Description](https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/en/home/services/ogd/portal.html)
- **[I14Y Interoperability Platform](https://www.i14y.admin.ch)**: National metadata catalog for data exchange between authorities
- **[Fedlex](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/)**: Swiss federal laws and regulations search platform
- **[Swiss National Bank (SNB)](https://data.snb.ch)**: Financial and monetary statistics, exchange rates

### Archives & Media

- **[Federal Archives](https://www.recherche.bar.admin.ch/recherche/#/en/search/simple)**: Historical Swiss documents since 1798, photos, films | Free/Paid
- **[Swiss National Library](https://www.helveticat.ch/)**: Helvetica collections catalog (OPAC)
- **[e-newspaperarchives](https://www.e-newspaperarchives.ch/)**: Digitized newspapers by National Library — ⚠️ 403 Error for some IPs
- **[DODIS](https://dodis.ch/)**: Swiss foreign policy documents since the mid-19th century (project covers Swiss foreign relations from 1848 onward)
- **[Notre Histoire](https://notrehistoire.ch/)**: Community historical photos and testimonials
- **[Swissdox](https://swissdox.ch/)**: Swiss media archive | Paid
- **[Swiss Media Database (SMD)](https://smd.ch/SMD)**: Press articles and TV reports | Professional access only
- **[Le Temps Archives](https://www.letemps.ch/)**: Archives of Gazette de Lausanne and Journal de Genève

### Courts

- **[Federal Tribunal](https://www.bger.ch)**: Swiss Federal Supreme Court judgments (searchable) | Free/Paid
- **[Federal Penal Tribunal](https://bstger.weblaw.ch)**: Federal criminal court decisions
- **[entscheidsuche.ch](https://entscheidsuche.ch)**: Meta-search for all Swiss court decisions across jurisdictions
- **[Federal Office of Justice](https://www.bj.admin.ch)**: Reports, legal opinions, decisions
- **[OFCOM Decisions](https://www.bakom.admin.ch)**: Broadcasting and telecommunications precedents
- **[IP Court Statistics](https://www.ige.ch)**: Intellectual property litigation data

### Stock

- **[SIX Swiss Exchange](https://www.six-group.com/en/products-services/the-swiss-stock-exchange.html)**: Official Swiss stock exchange

## Legal Entities

**Business registries, company information, and commercial intelligence.**

### Central Business Registry

- **[ZEFIX](https://www.zefix.ch)**: Central Business Names Index — aggregates data from all 26 Swiss cantonal commercial registers
- **[Swiss Official Gazette of Commerce (SHAB)](https://shab.ch/)**: Commercial register changes, bankruptcy notices, litigation

### Commercial Databases

- **[Moneyhouse](https://www.moneyhouse.ch)**: Business information, credit reports, company monitoring | Freemium
- **[OpenCorporates — Switzerland](https://opencorporates.com/companies/ch)**: International open database of Swiss companies (1M+ entries)

### Other

- **[SwissReg](https://www.swissreg.ch)**: Intellectual property database (trademarks, patents, designs)
- **[Money in Politics](https://moneyinpolitics.ch)**: Political financing data

## Vehicles

**Vehicle registration, license plate lookup, and transportation records.**

> **Privacy Warning**: Swiss vehicle owner data is publicly accessible but regulated. Some cantons require justification or charge fees. Misuse for stalking/harassment is illegal.

### Land Vehicles — License Plate Databases

#### Easy Way
- [Swiss Plates Autoindex Chrome Extension](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ch.tasoulesplaques)

#### Registers
- [e-Autoindex](https://www.eautoindex.ch/)

### Aviation

- **[Swiss Aircraft Register](https://www.bazl.admin.ch)**: Civil aviation aircraft registration database

### Maritime & Railways

- **[National Vehicle Register (Railways)](https://www.rollingstockregister.ch)**: Railway vehicle registration
- **Ship Registration**: Pleasure craft and commercial vessels are registered through the federal **Swiss Maritime Navigation Office (SMNO)** and, for inland watercraft, through cantonal authorities. Access requirements vary — contact the relevant authority directly.

## People, Phones, Social

**Individual person searches, professional registries, and contact directories.**

### Professional Registries

- **[MedReg — Register of Medical Professions](https://healthreg-public.admin.ch)**: Official federal register (FOPH/BAG) covering all physicians, dentists, pharmacists, veterinarians, chiropractors, and midwives practising in Switzerland. Mandatory registration since 1 January 2018. Includes PsyReg (psychologists) and BetReg (establishments).
- **[FMH Doctor Registry](https://doctorfmh.ch/en/)**: FMH membership directory of Swiss doctors (reflects FMH association members; for the authoritative legal register, use MedReg above)
- **[Psychologist Registry](https://www.psychologie.ch/psychologensuche)**: Licensed psychologists in Switzerland
- **[Federal Administration Directory (Staatskalender)](https://www.staatskalender.admin.ch/home)**: Directory of Swiss federal government employees, authorities, and institutions

### Phone & Contact Directories

- **[Tel.Search](https://tel.search.ch/index.en.html)**: Swiss phone numbers and addresses
- **[Local.ch](https://www.local.ch/)**: Swiss phone directory and business listings
- **[OSINT Industries](https://osint.industries)**: Multi-source OSINT lookup tool (email/username/phone)

### Business Directories

- **[Swiss Yellow Pages (local.ch)](https://www.local.ch/)**: Business directory and services
- **[Branchenbuch](http://branchenbuch.ch/)**: Business directory platform — ⚠️ Redirect loop reported

## Public Procurements

**Government contracts, tenders, and public spending.**

- **[SIMAP](https://www.simap.ch)**: Official Swiss public procurement platform. Mandatory publication threshold: CHF 230,000 for supplies and services; CHF 2 million for civil engineering. Covers federal, cantonal, and municipal authorities.
- **[Federal Finance Administration (EFV)](https://www.efv.admin.ch)**: Swiss federal budget and financial data
- **[ARAMIS Research Database](https://www.aramis.admin.ch)**: Federal research projects and evaluations information system

## WHOIS

**Domain registration and DNS information for Swiss (.ch) and Liechtenstein (.li) domains.**

- **[Switch Registry](https://www.switch.ch)**: Official .ch and .li domain registry
- **[RDDS (WHOIS) Service](https://www.switch.ch/whois/)**: Domain registration data (personal info restricted since January 2021 due to privacy regulations)
- **[Federal Office of Communications](https://www.bakom.admin.ch/en/ch-internet-domain-names)**: .ch internet domain policy and regulations

> **Note**: Personal information of .ch domain holders is no longer publicly accessible via WHOIS since January 2021 due to Swiss data protection regulations.

## Usage Guidelines

### Legal Compliance

- **Swiss Data Protection Act (revDSG/FADP)** — applies to all personal data processing in Switzerland (revised law in force since September 2023)
- **GDPR compliance** — required when processing data of EU residents
- Many services have **legitimate interest requirements**
- **Commercial use** may require separate licensing

> **Legal Notice**: This collection is for educational and legitimate investigative purposes only. Users must comply with Swiss privacy laws, the revised FADP, GDPR where applicable, and the terms of service of individual platforms. Always verify access requirements before use.
